How to Play

Master the physics-based combat with these simple controls

Controls

Player 1

Arrow Keys: Move your character
Space: Jump

Player 2

WASD: Move your character
Shift: Jump

Objective

Push your opponent off the platform or get on top of them to win each round!

Game Mechanics

Physics System

Realistic physics makes every match unpredictable. Use momentum and gravity to your advantage.

Timing & Strategy

Perfect your jumps and pushes. Sometimes the best defense is a well-timed dodge!

Winning

First to win 3 rounds is the match winner. Win by being on top when the timer runs out or by knocking your opponent off.
